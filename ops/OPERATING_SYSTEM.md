# CogCage Operating System

## Mission
Build, launch, and monetize CogCage fast enough to remain cashflow-positive under fixed bankroll constraints.

## Priority Stack
1. Cashflow
2. Retention
3. Product depth
4. Aesthetic polish

## Work Selection Rule
Before starting any task, write:
- Hypothesis:
- KPI impacted:
- Time to ship:
- Expected outcome in 7 days:

If unclear, do not do it.

## Daily Minimum Ship
Ship at least one of:
- Conversion improvement
- Monetization feature
- Distribution asset
- Retention mechanic

## End-of-Day Protocol
Log in `ops/logs/YYYY-MM-DD.md`:
- What shipped
- KPI movement
- Revenue impact
- Blockers
- Next top task
