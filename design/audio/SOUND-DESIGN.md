# SOUND DESIGN

## Direction

The Molt Pit exists at the bottom of a digital ocean. Sound reflects this: pressure, weight, distance. Bioluminescent things don't screech — they pulse and hum. Combat is physical and wet and occasionally electrical.

The humor has a sound. BUZZ crackles. THE APOLOGIST apologizes. PAPER-MACHÉ crumples. The sound design is part of the joke.

**Tonal reference:**
- Wet organic impacts (not dry metal clangs)
- Deep ocean pressure ambience for The Brine
- Arcade punctuation for UI feedback
- Heavy hydraulics for heavy Carapaces
- Electrical discharge for BUZZ and shock effects
- Ancient stone/resonance for Legendary items
