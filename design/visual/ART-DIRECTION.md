# ART DIRECTION

## What We Are

Cartoon violence with genuine menace. Think: the Saturday morning cartoon that aired right before the watershed. Everything is expressive and slightly absurd, but the Pit is real and losing costs something.

**Primary references:**
- **High on Life** — cel-shaded organic-mechanical hybrid aesthetic. Weapons feel alive. The world has texture.
- **Borderlands** — thick outlines, expressive characters, bold color blocking. Everything has personality.
- **Pacific Rim** — scale and weight. The mechs feel heavy. Combat has consequence.
- **Deep sea bioluminescence** — the color language of The Brine. Cyan, violet, deep ocean blue. Things that glow in the dark.

## What We Are Not

- Realistic. Never photorealistic.
- Grimdark for its own sake. The darkness serves the humor.
- Generic sci-fi blue-grey. That's not The Brine. That's an airport.
- Cute. Adjacent to cute but never cute.

## The Lobster

Mechanized crustacean. Lobster anatomy is real and present — carapace, claws, antennae, rostrum, uropod tail fan — but everything is armored, articulated, and slightly too large. Think: if a lobster was designed by someone who had only ever heard lobsters described over the phone, and that person also worked in military hardware.

Eyes: stalked compound eyes with emissive glow. They have opinions.

## The Arena

Dark. Pressurized. Bioluminescent. The floor is a perspective grid that extends into blackness. The lighting comes from the combatants, not the environment. There is no ceiling visible. There may not be one.

## Color Language

See `COLOR-SYSTEM.md` for full palette.

- **Cyan `#00E5FF`** — The Brine, tech, neutral UI
- **Yellow `#FFD600`** — Legendary, The House, important
- **Red `#FF1744`** — Danger, Berserker rank, damage
- **Green `#00C853`** — HP, regeneration, connected
- **Purple `#9C27B0`** — Rare, phase effects, The Ghost
- **Dark `#050510`** — The Brine substrate, backgrounds

## Item Iconography Direction

See `ICONOGRAPHY.md`.

Each item icon is:
- 64×64px minimum, scalable vector preferred
- Bold outline (3px at 64px)
- Single dominant color matching item's color hex
- Readable at 32×32px
- No text in icon
- Personality visible at a glance — MAXINE looks industrial, THE FLICKER looks fast, ORACLE looks ancient
